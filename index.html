Privacy Policy for Gookie Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 29 August 2025    Last Updated: 29 August 2025

Gookie ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use the Gookie Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Extension, we may collect the following information:

  • LinkedIn Profile Information: Name, job title, and other publicly visible details from the LinkedIn profile page you visit.
  • Website Content: Content from the active tab when the Extension is used, to identify relevant details for generating llm response.
  • User Preferences: Settings and configurations saved in Chrome Storage (e.g., API token, email template preferences).
  • Authentication Data: If you log in with your Google account, we collect basic profile details for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized cold email drafts based on LinkedIn profile data.
  • Provide a consistent and customized user experience.
  • Improve the performance and functionality of the Extension.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.
  • Data may be sent securely to our backend service at 'https://api.gookie.xyz' for processing and returning email drafts.
  • We do not share data for advertising, analytics, or unrelated purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • User preferences are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API.
  • Data sent to our servers is transmitted over HTTPS and processed securely.
  • We retain only the data necessary for the intended functionality and do not store LinkedIn profile data permanently.

5. Your Choices

  • You can remove the Extension at any time to stop data collection.
  • You can request deletion of any stored data related to your account by contacting us at dwi.ritik20@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension may use third-party APIs (e.g., Google Identity API) strictly for authentication purposes. These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
